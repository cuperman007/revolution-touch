Revolution Touch

A group of touch lovers in the midlands who are passionate about progressing in the world of touch rugby.

Each player has an appetite and commitment to becoming an elite player and want to promote a progression pathway for all players.

Our coaching staff are all experienced players and coaches in their own right. All have played Touch to a high level and have represented at Regional Nationals and for England Teams.

We are lucky to have an England appointed Coach and many who coach for West Wildcats Regional Squads.

We run coaching sessions for all abilities from our base at Redditch Rugby Club

We enter competitions at all levels and age groups.

FAQs

How much does membership / training cost?

£3 per session or £10 per month, payable via bank transfer.

First session is free

I already play for another team, can I also play / train with you?

Yes, everyone is welcome.

What ages can train and play?

All players aged 14+ are welcome.

Juniors must have an responsible adult playing with them, or pitchside at all times.

Do I need to be a registered ETA member?

No.
